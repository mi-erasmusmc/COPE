Please make sure that the input is admissible:

  • Age is a number between 0 and 100 years
  • Respiratory rate is a number between 10 and 60 breaths per minute
  • Lactate dehydrogenase (LDH) level is a number between 100 and 1000 units per liter (U/L)
  • C-reactive protein (CRP) level is a number between 1 and 500 milligrams per liter (mg/L)
  • Serum albumin is a number between 10 and 60 grams per liter (g/L)
  • Serum urea is a number between 1 and 80 millimoles per liter (mmol/L)
